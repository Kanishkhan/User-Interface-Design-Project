Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Global Delivery: Shipping Beyond Borders

At NRGK Fashion, we’re proud to offer international shipping to most countries across the globe. Whether you’re in New York, Tokyo, or Sydney, our goal is to bring our stylish products right to your doorstep. Here’s what you need to know:

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods to accommodate our customers' preferences. These include major credit cards such as Visa, Mastercard, American Express, as well as popular online payment platforms like PayPal and Apple Pay. Additionally, we offer secure checkout options for a seamless transaction experience.

How long does shipping take?

Delivery times depend on your location and the shipping method you choose. Expedited options are available for those who can’t wait to flaunt their new NRGK fashion finds.

Shipping Fees:

Shipping fees vary based on your destination. Rest assured, we strive to keep these costs as reasonable as possible. Keep an eye out for any special promotions or discounts on shipping fees!

Smooth Process:

Our dedicated logistics team ensures a seamless delivery process. You’ll receive tracking information so you can follow your package’s journey.

Customer Satisfaction:

Your satisfaction matters to us. If you have any questions or concerns about shipping, don’t hesitate to reach out.

What is your return policy?

At NRGK, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, you may return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Please ensure that the item is in its original condition with all tags attached. For detailed instructions on how to initiate a return, please visit our Returns & Exchanges page.

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely. NRGK is committed to offering only authentic and genuine luxury clothing and branded items. We source our products directly from authorized distributors and reputable suppliers to guarantee their authenticity. Each item undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our high standards before being made available for purchase on our website.
